Xenia - μεγαλοπρέπεια
At Grandeur Hotel, we believe in02 the power of hospitality to create meaningful connections with our guests. Inspired by the Greek concept of Xenia, the art of extending guest-friendship to strangers, we strive to provide an experience that goes beyond luxury accommodation.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that great food and warm hospitality can transform a hotel into a home away from home. Drawing inspiration from the culinary traditions of Greece, we offer a cuisine fit for the gods, prepared with the freshest local ingredients and served with a smile. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every detail, from the inviting fireplace in our lobby to the hand-picked flowers that adorn our guest rooms.

When you stay with us, you'll experience the warmth and tradition of ages-old hospitality in a setting that is both grand and intimate. At Grandeur Hotel, we are driven by a passion for creating unforgettable memories. We invite you to join us and discover the magic of Xenia for yourself.

Samantha Miller

Managing Partner

Samantha Miller, who has been leading the grandeur since 2000, believes that running a restaurant is primarily about catering to people's needs. This includes taking care of both the staff and guests, ensuring everyone feels at ease and welcome. When restaurant employees are treated with respect and appreciation, they take pride in their work and derive job satisfaction, ultimately resulting in a positive experience for the guests.

While Samantha may be soft-spoken and charming (qualities she attributes to her Southern upbringing), she is extremely driven. Her dedicated, hands-on approach to management ensures the high standards of service and hospitality at the restaurant have stayed consistent for over 15 years. Under miller,Grandeur’s refreshing approach to service emphasizes the emotional elements of hospitality: Have a good time and above all, make sure the guests have a great time.

After years of managing high-end restaurants in New York City, Samantha Miller made her way to the West Coast. Her first position in San Francisco was working as a General Manager alongside renowned Bay Area Chef, Alice Waters, at Chez Panisse. After spending several years there, she joined the team at The Slanted Door, where she gained a deep understanding and appreciation for the nuances of Vietnamese cuisine. In 2000, Miller assumed leadership of the grandeur, bringing with her a wealth of experience and knowledge in fine dining. Her commitment to excellence and attention to detail have made the grandeur a standout establishment in the San Francisco culinary scene.

Ryan Lee

Chef/Partner

Ryan Lee is an acclaimed chef whose culinary talents have earned him a reputation as one of the most sought-after chefs in the industry. Ryan was born and raised in Seoul, South Korea, where he grew up surrounded by a rich and diverse culinary tradition. His passion for cooking began at a young age, as he would spend hours in the kitchen with his mother, learning to prepare traditional Korean dishes that have become the foundation of his cooking style.

Ryan's specialty lies in fusion cuisine, which he has perfected over years of experimentation and dedication. He combines traditional Korean flavors with contemporary techniques to create dishes that are both innovative and delicious. His signature dish, a Korean-inspired surf-and-turf, is a perfect example of his unique approach to cooking. This dish features tender, marinated short ribs served alongside succulent lobster tail, all topped with a spicy gochujang glaze that perfectly balances sweet and heat.

Today, Ryan is a successful chef, entrepreneur, and culinary educator. He has established himself as a leader in the industry and continues to inspire aspiring chefs to pursue their dreams. With his innovative approach to cooking and his commitment to using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients, Ryan Lee is a true culinary trailblazer and a shining example of what can be achieved with hard work, passion, and determination.
