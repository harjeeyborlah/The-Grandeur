Grandeur: Modernistic Hungarian Taste

Long before the terms "friss", "szezonális", "kézműves" és "helyi" became buzzwords among food enthusiasts, the Hungarians had already developed a rich and unique cuisine. Their meals were made from the freshest ingredients available, such as hearty meats, fresh vegetables, and fragrant herbs from the countryside

Goulash, Dobos Torte, Chimney Cake, Tokaji Aszú. These are just a few examples of the mouth-watering dishes that evoke fond memories of Hungarian cuisine. The rich flavors and unique textures of tender beef and paprika, layered cakes with caramelized sugar, sweet doughnuts covered in cinnamon sugar, and the complex notes of Tokaji wine are a testament to Hungary's culinary heritage.

As Hungary's culinary scene continues to thrive, some restaurants have become institutions known for their warm hospitality and unforgettable dining experiences. These grandeur founders have welcomed strangers as friends for over a decade, offering a taste of Hungarian cuisine that lingers in the memory long after the meal is over. Now, they invite you to recreate some of their favorite dishes in your own kitchen, so you can savor the flavors of Hungary wherever you are. And as they say in Greece, where hospitality is also highly valued, "kali orexi" – wishing you a good appetite!
